Patent PDW @ AOM 2024

Patent Data for Research PDW @ AOM 2024

I will be co-hosting the next Patent Research Data Professional Development Workshop (PDW) "Advances in Patent Research: New Data, Measures, and Methods" at the 2024 Academy of Management (AOM) Annual Meeting in Chicago (IL, USA), 9-13 August 2024, together with Lucy Xiaolu Wang (UMass). The workshop takes place on Saturday, August 10, 2024, 11.30 AM - 2.00 PM CT at the Sheraton Grand Chicago in room "Erie".

Aim of the PDW session is to bring together leading scholars from economics and strategy/management to discuss recent advances in patent research and related data set useful for empirical work on innovation and R&D from diverse and international perspectives.

Here is our extraordinary lineup of distinguished speakers and discussants:

  • Matt Marx (Cornell University) will present "New, Open Datasets for Innovation Research".
  • Paola Criscuolo (Imperial College London) will share insights on "Invention Disclosure and Patent Renewal Processes Inside a Large ICT Multinational Company".
  • Tony Tong (University of Colorado Boulder) will talk about "Patent Acquisition and Monetization Under Invalidation Risk: Evidence from the America Invents Act".

Presentations will be followed with discussions by:

  • Andrew Toole (USPTO)
  • Jeff Furman (Boston University)
