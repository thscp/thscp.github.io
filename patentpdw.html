Patent Data for Research PDW @ AOM

I am co-hosting the Patent PDW "Advances in Patent Research: New Data, Measures, and Methods" at the 2025 Academy of Management (AOM) Annual Meeting in Copenhagen, Denmark, 25-29 July 2025, together with Mingtao Xu (Tsinghua University).

The workshop will take place Friday, July 25, 2025, 08.30 AM - 11.00 AM CEST (GMT+2) at the Bella Center: Hall D-D1-m4.

Aim of the PDW session is to bring together leading scholars from economics and strategy/management to discuss recent advances in patent research and related data set useful for empirical work on innovation and R&D from diverse and international perspectives.

Here is our extraordinary lineup of distinguished speakers and discussants:

  • David H. Hsu (UPenn Wharton) will present "Novel Ways of Using Patent Data in Management Research".
  • Karin Hoisl (University of Mannheim & CBS) will share insights on "Filling the Gap: Consequences of Collaborator Loss in Corporate R&D--updated and considerably changed version".
