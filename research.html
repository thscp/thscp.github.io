Research

Welcome to my home page. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Managment, Strategy and Innovation (MSI) at KU Leuven. My research is funded by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO).

My research interests are in the economics of innovation, science and technology. My on-going projects are about human capital externalities in scientific production, digitization and the impact of institutions on knowledge spillovers.

I am on the 2020/2021 job market.
