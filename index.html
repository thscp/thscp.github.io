Welcome to my home page. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in business economics at the Department of Managment, Strategy and Innovation (MSI) at KU Leuven, Faculty of Economics and Business. My research is funded by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO).

My research interests are in the economics of innovation, science and technology. My ongoing work is about human capital externalities in scientific production, digitization and the impact of institutions on knowledge spillovers.
