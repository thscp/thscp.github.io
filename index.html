About me

Welcome to my home page. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in economics at the Technical University of Munich. I received my Ph.D. from KU Leuven in 2021.

My research is supported by the German Research Foundation (DFG) and was previously funded by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO).

My research interests are in the economics of innovation, science and technology. In on-going projects, I investigate the impact of science funding, competition and human capital externalities in scientific production, digitization, and the role of institutions for knowledge spillovers.
