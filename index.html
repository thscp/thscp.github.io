About me

Welcome to my home page. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Management, Strategy and Innovation (MSI) at KU Leuven. My research is funded by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO).

My research interests are in the economics of innovation, science and technology. In on-going projects, I investigate human capital externalities in scientific production, digitization and the impact of institutions on knowledge spillovers.

Starting from October 1st 2021, I will be joining the Department of Economics & Policy at TUM School of Management as a post-doctoral researcher.
