About me

Welcome to my home page. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in economics at the Technical University of Munich. I received my Ph.D. from KU Leuven in 2021.

My research is supported by the German Research Foundation (DFG) and was previously funded by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO).

I am also currently holder of an Add-on Fellowship for Interdisciplinary Economics from the Joachim Herz Foundation.

My research interests are in the economics of science and innovation. In on-going projects, I investigate the impact of human capital externalities, science funding and competition on scientific production, digitization, and how institutions affect knowledge spillovers.
